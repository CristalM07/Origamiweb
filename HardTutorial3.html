Step 1: Base Folds
Start with a square piece of paper. Fold and unfold diagonally in both directions to create an “X” crease. Then, fold the sides to the center, forming a diamond shape (rabbit-ear fold on both sides).
Step 2: First Triangle Shape
Complete the rabbit ear fold on both sides, then repeat the fold for the other flap. You now have a fish base.
Step 3:Form the Ears
Mountain fold one flap behind, fold the other flap behind as well. Fold the tip behind, and then fold both corners behind the structure.
Step 4: Shape the Face
Fold the entire structure in half. Fold the flaps outward to shape the ears. Repeat the same fold for the other flap.
Step 5: Finish the Bunny
Push open the flaps to shape both ears. Perform reverse folds on the front (for the nose) and the back (for the tail). The mouse is complete!
