Step 1
Start with a square piece of origami paper. Make sure it is placed flat on the surface with the colored side facing up.
Step 2
Fold the paper diagonally in half to form a triangle. Crease the fold sharply, then unfold.
Step 3
Fold the bottom corners of the triangle up to meet at the top point. This creates a smaller triangle.
Step 4
Rotate the paper slightly and fold each corner of the new triangle into the center point. This will make additional creases.
Step 5
Repeat the process of folding the outer edges into the center point. This step further compresses the paper into a more compact shape.
Step 6
Continue folding each corner to the center. At this point, the origami piece will have several folds leading to a symmetric shape.
