Step 1: Prepare Your Paper
Begin with a square sheet of origami paper, colored side facing down.
Step 2: Make the First Fold
Fold the paper diagonally from one corner to the opposite corner, creating a triangle. Crease the fold and then unfold it back to a square.
Step 3: Create Creases
Fold one corner of the square towards the center point. Crease the fold well.
Step 4: Create Creases
Fold the opposite corner to the center. Then fold the remaining two corners, so all four corners meet in the center.
Step 5: Create Creases
Once all corners are folded, fold the new small triangles again towards the center to form sharper creases.
Step 6: Create Creases
Fold the paper lengthwise down the middle, creating a longer triangular shape. Press down to keep the folds in place.
Step 6: Create Creases
Refold along the long crease and shape the pointed end of the plane. Ensure all creases are firm for the airplane to hold its shape.
