Origami Tutorials
Easy Medium Expert
Step 1:Top left
Begin with a triangle-shaped piece of paper. Fold the top edge down, making a small loop (which will be part of the bird's head or neck).
Step 2:Top middle
Fold the paper in half along the dashed line, bringing the right side over to the left. You now have a smaller triangle.
Step 3: Top right
Fold one of the triangle's sides upwards to form an angle, creating what looks like a bird's head or neck. The tip should be pointing upwards.
Step 4: Bottom middle
Fold the two lower sections outward in opposite directions to make what could be the bird's wings.
Step 5: Create Creases
Adjust the head or neck of the bird by bending the tip downwards slightly.
