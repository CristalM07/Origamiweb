Step 1: Base fold
Start with a square sheet of paper. Fold it in half diagonally to form a triangle. Fold the right and left corners up toward the top point, forming a kite shape. Then fold the outer flaps inwards along the existing creases to create a smaller kite shape.
Step 2:Repeat and Assemble Modules
Repeat the same fold pattern to create 8 identical units in total. Once you have the units, begin assembling by tucking the smaller flaps of one unit into the pockets of the next, creating pairs (you will need 4 pairs).
